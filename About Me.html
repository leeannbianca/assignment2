About Me

Hi! My name is Lee-Anne Tlhatsoane, I was born on the 13th of September in the year 2000 to
Susan and Piet Tlhatsoane. I am an african woman born and raised in Kimberley Nothern Cape.
I am the eldest of 4, my parents have been married for over 24 years, and we are a very close
family.

I attended Homevale High School, a public school in Homelite Extension, I finished my matric
in 2018 and in 2019 I enrolled into Sol Plaatje University, where I started my ICT career. This
will be my 4th year as I will be doing my Advanced Diploma. My graduation ceremony will be
held in May, I am extremely greatfull and blessed to have come this far.

I learned a lot over the past 3 years, I met amasing, innovative and inspiring human beings,
and I couldn't be more greatful. My experience has taught me one fundamental thing - life is
unpredictable. It might be good, bad, weird, and sometimes it might not interest you so trust
me when I say, College life is preparation for all of this. It is the perfect combination of joy
and hardships.
